Design Principles

Adelyn Smith

Visual Hierarchy

Church of Jesus Christ

Church of Jesus Christ

The organization of this page represents Visual Hierarchy. The focus of the page falls onto a large image representing the main message of the website. There are two smaller images adjacent to the large one, but when viewed on a smaller device, the smaller images and links fall into place of importance for the viewers, while the central images are still the primary focus of the page.

Proximity

Amazon

Amazon

The elements on the Amazon website are all in close proximity to one another. For this website, it is a selling tactic. All of the blocks together let the users view multiple products immediately when the site loads. This creates connections and a direct relationship between the elements.

Use of White Space

Typing.com

Typing.com

The home page for Typing.com has lots of space between each of the elements of the page, thus creating an open site. Each element is clear to the user and enhances their accessibility and experience of the website.
