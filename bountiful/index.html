Carlsbad California has up to 7 miles of coastline and a great selection of fun activities. Here in Carlsbad, there is shopping, a great golfing course, restaurants that offer fresh foods supplied by Bountiful Foods. For about 60 years, the tradition of The Flower Fields has grown into a significant display of Mother Nature. LEGOLAND California is a theme park centered around Legos. It also holds a display of sea life in its aquarium. Carlsbad is also well known for the great areas for surfing. In June 2023, Carlsbad is hosting the 2023 USA Surfing Championships.
