About Us

Bountiful Foods promotes healthy lifestyles while using locally grown produce to create healthy snacks for you. Founded in Carlsbad, California in 1945 by Franny Johnson, this is a family central business. Franny sold fresh fruits and vegetables in a stand at a local Farmer's Market. It has grown from that stand to serve not only those locally but expand and serve across California and the United States. From food trucks, fruit baskets, and fruit drinks Bountiful Foods is an advocate for healthy eating.

The produce is collected locally as well as in some national and international locations. In order to make it the most healthy option for customers, there are organic options. Bountiful Foods is also the produce supplier for local favorite restaurants such as Campfire, Bluewater Grill, and Fresco.
